The Bag of Joy Project is a service project start-up by 2 Diocesan Boys' School students who want to deliver joy to lives of those who are facing a tough time during the epidemic by delivering Bags of Joy to them. We are asking for donations in the form of supplies. The collected Bag of Joy will be distributed to children and elderlies in need through community centres and direct deliveries.
